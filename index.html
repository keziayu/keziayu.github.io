Hello There

Just here to share a little bit about my trip to Vancouver and some gems that I recommend for you to check out! Here is a list of things that we did in 6 days.

  • Activities
    • Stawamus Chief Trail. Located in Stawamus Chief Provincial Park and Protected Area. A hike with 3 peaks and 3 different views. Level: Intermediate-Difficult
    • Dog Mountain Trail at Mount Seymour Provincial Park. Dog friendly, kid friendly, overall an easy, chill hike.
    • Driving Range at Seymour Creek Golf Centre. Cheap fun date idea. Golf club rentals available for lefties too!
    • Sea to Sky Gondola. On the pricier side, a $50 round trip gondola ride over Howe Sound. Well worth it as there's a suspension bridge, more hiking trails,a restaurant and drinks!
    • SUP at Jericho Beach. Stand-Up Paddleboard in the English Bay surrounded by mountains. Great sunset view and we even spotted two seals!
  • Sustenance
    • Stevenston Pizza Co. is well known among locals. Can never go wrong with pizza, but Stevenston Pizza is definietly the right way to go!
    • Sunny Chibas Fried Chicken. Mexican restaurant along the Sea-to-Sky Highway. A scenic route to a delicious meal.
