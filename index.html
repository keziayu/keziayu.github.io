  • Activities
    • Stawamus Chief Trail:Located at Stawamus Chief Provincial Park and Protected Area. A hike with 3 peaks and 3 different views. Level: Intermediate-Difficult
    • Dog Mountain Trail: Located at Mount Seymour Provincial Park. Dog friendly, kid friendly, overall an easy, chill hike.
    • Driving Range at Seymour Creek Golf Centre: Cheap fun date and golf club rentals available for lefties too!
    • Sea to Sky Gondola: On the pricier side, a $50 round trip gondola ride over Howe Sound. Well worth it as there's a suspension bridge, more hiking trails,a restaurant and drinks!
    • SUP at Jericho Beach: Stand-Up Paddleboard in the English Bay surrounded by mountains. Great sunset view and we even spotted two seals!
  • Sustenance
    • Steveston Pizza Co. is well known among locals. Can never go wrong with pizza, but Stevenston Pizza is definietly the right way to go!
    • Sunny Chibas Fried Chicken: Restaurant along the Sea-to-Sky Highway. A scenic route to a delicious meal.
